Understanding the Wove Score

Every product you scan receives a letter grade from A+ to F. Our proprietary algorithm analyzes multiple factors to give you an instant, easy-to-understand sustainability rating.

A+/A

Excellent

Natural fibers, certified sustainable, minimal chemical treatment. Safe for sensitive skin and the environment.

B/C

Good to Moderate

Mixed natural and synthetic fibers with some chemical treatments. Acceptable for most uses but not ideal for sensitive skin.

D

Poor

High synthetic content with chemical treatments. Reduced breathability and increased microplastic concerns.

F

Avoid

Primarily synthetic fabrics. Non-breathable, high microplastic shedding, and significant environmental impact.

What we Analyze

  • Percentage of natural vs. synthetic fibers
  • Third-party certifications (GOTS, OEKO-TEX, RWS, GRS)
  • Chemical treatments and finishes
  • Microplastic shedding risk
  • PFAS and harmful chemical concerns
  • Breathability and skin safety
  • Overall environmental sustainability

Our detailed scoring methodology is proprietary. Each factor is weighted based on extensive research and industry standards to provide you with the most accurate sustainability assessment.
